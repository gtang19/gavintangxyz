There are a lot of parties involved in healthcare and we can’t get rid of them, but can we make them play nice? There’s what seems to be a constant battle between providers and insurance, is there any way to tone down the fighting a little bit?

Specifications

Cleveland Clinic as a major hospital has more leverage than an individual patient. It’s in the best interest of both the hospital and insurance company to negotiate hard, but this hurts the patient. Theoretically, both parties should benefit from serving the best interests of the patient. Is there a way Cleveland Clinic can negotiate a less antagonistic relationship between players involved in healthcare?
